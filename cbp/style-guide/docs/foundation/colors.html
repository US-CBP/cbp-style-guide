Color Palette

This palette is designed to create a consistent look and feel across products. The palette is influenced by the official Department of Homeland Security brand colors, with bright saturated tints of blue and red, grounded in sophisticated deeper shades of cool blues and grays. These colors — combined with clear hierarchy, good information design, and ample white space — should aid users in ease of use and quick navigation. This is a simple, minimalist color palette. Shades of blue dominate, providing a neutral backdrop on which brighter shades, clean type treatment, and bright white content areas “pop” on the page.
